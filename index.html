Three Visitor Paths

Every visitor lands in one of three buckets. The site's job is to figure out which one and move them forward without friction.

Entry Points

Organic Search (SEO)
Paid Ads / Campaigns
Direct / Referral
Trade Shows

Visitor Buckets

Intent

Ready to talk. Active buying process.

Signals:
  • Visits product pages multiple times
  • Downloads specs/brochures
  • Searches for pricing/quote
  • Comes from high-intent keywords
Key Pages:
  • Product Detail
  • Request Quote
  • Book a Call
  • Find a Rep

Interest

Researching. Early-stage evaluation.

Signals:
  • Reading category/industry pages
  • Watching videos
  • Comparing solutions
  • First-time visitor exploring
Key Pages:
  • Industry Landings
  • Solutions Hub
  • Video Library
  • Playbooks/Guides (gated)

Aftermarket

Existing customer. Service needs.

Signals:
  • Returns to site repeatedly
  • Searches for parts/support
  • Known customer in Salesforce
  • Bookmarked aftermarket pages
Key Pages:
  • Aftermarket Hub
  • Parts Request
  • Service Request
  • Training (PMMI)

Primary Conversions

Book a Call <15 min response
Download Playbook Email nurture
Parts / Service Request Support ticket

Streamlining Opportunities

  • Persistent CTAs: "Book a Call" sticky in header, context-aware on every page
  • Smart routing: Industry detected from entry page → personalized product recommendations
  • Reduce clicks: Product Detail pages should have inline quote form, not just link
  • Aftermarket shortcut: "I'm an existing customer" link in nav/footer → direct to Aftermarket Hub
  • Exit intent: Playbook offer for Interest visitors about to leave
